Why Trucking?

Truck drivers are essential! Having a career in the commercial industry is a desirable career that is in high demand, as well as having job stability. Most truck drivers don't like to call what they do for a living a job, instead they call it a lifestyle. Many truckers who own their own rig love adding custom styles and upgrading the interior for maximum living on the road. Many truckers are team drivers as well... Husband and wife, buddies or just teaming up to save money day to day... They enjoy travelling together working and making an adventure!

What is interstate freight?

Interstate means you would be hauling freight to and from more than one state. You will be seeing many places... So how would you like to travel to different cities & states throughout North America, including Canada and Mexico? The training is not long and you can be on your way to job security, a great salary and even an early retirement!

What is intrastate freight?

Intrastate means you will be hauling freight within one state, travelling to local cities, the open roads of the plains and the picturesque countryside. These routes are many times going to the same locations which you would become quite familiar with the roads. Local driving jobs are also in demand as well...

Why Drive a Bus?

The bussing industry is always in search of drivers. Bus drivers are beneficial for the transportation service industry. Most bus drivers are employed by various industries of transportation. From Charter Busses to Urban Transit, from School bussing to Services, from Incidental to Transportation. You can play a vital role as a part-time, full-time or coming out of retirement position. Your career awaits...

What to expect from us?

Diamond Trucking School, Inc. will be teaching & guiding you to become a trucker who will practice safety first, defensive driving, handling your rig with driving skills in all weather, from rain to snow, from mountain to countryside, to city to downtown traffic. You will become comfortable behind the wheel and feel at ease when you're out on the road.
