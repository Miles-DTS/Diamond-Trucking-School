ABOUT DIAMOND

This company was primarily formed for the very reason the founder noticed a need for thorough hands on and safety based training in the transportation industry. Our founder spent nearly a decade over the road and witnessed thousands of unsafe situations that comprehensive driver training would have prevented those dangerous and sometimes costly situations. The founder is a Washington State CDL Examiner which would clearly benefit students at Diamond Trucking School Inc.



Diamond Trucking School Inc. will also provide high-quality CDL training and certification to veterans. We facilitate job placement and create partnerships with trucking & bus companies committed to hiring our graduates. We have relationships with several organizations that foster a supportive community that offers mentorship, counseling, and resources to help veterans transition into civilian life. Empowering veterans to become self-sufficient by providing financial assistance for housing, transportation, and other essential needs during the training period.

Diamond Trucking School Inc. shall comply with all State, Federal, and Local laws (including the Americans with Disabilities Act, Title VII of the Civil Rights Act, Age Discrimination, Employment Acts) as to treatment and compensation.

OUR SERVICES

The services offered at Trucking Diamond School Inc. will consist of a range of programs tailored to meet the diverse needs of our students. Our services will include Training Programs that cover both theoretical knowledge and practical skills. These programs will prepare students for the CDL exams and provide hands-on experience with driving several types of commercial vehicles, which includes specialized endorsement courses such as hazardous materials (HAZMAT) and passenger transportation.

The students will learn commercial transportation, defensive driving, regulatory compliance and planning, a comprehensive CDL Training Program that includes classroom instruction, hands-on practice, practice tests, simulated driving experiences, customized training plans, regarding each individual abilities and circumstances.

OUR VISION

To provide quality and a high standard education for students who have dreams of freedom on the road to success, after all, “Road Education gets you to your Destination,” whether it’s starting your own business or providing top-quality service to the industry.

OUR MISSION

Empower aspiring truck & bus drivers with the education and safety skills required to succeed in the ever-growing and in-demand field of commercial trucking. We are committed to providing a safe, and inclusive learning environment that fosters personal growth and professional development.
