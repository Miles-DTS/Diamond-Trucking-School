About Diamond Trucking School

This company was primarily formed for the very reason the founder noticed a need for thorough hands-on and safety-based training in the transportation industry.

Our founder spent nearly a decade over the road and witnessed thousands of unsafe situations that comprehensive driver training would have prevented. The founder is a Washington State CDL Examiner which would clearly benefit students at Diamond Trucking School Inc.

Our Mission

Empower aspiring truck & bus drivers with the education and safety skills required to succeed in the ever-growing and in-demand field of commercial trucking.

Our Vision

To provide quality and a high standard education for students who have dreams of freedom on the road to success.
